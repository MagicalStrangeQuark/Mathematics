<h6>Chain Rule</h6>

A method for finding the __derivative__ of a __composition__ of __functions__.

The formula is $\frac{d}{dx}f[g(x)]=f'[g(x)]g'(x)$.

Another form of the chain rule is $\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{dy}{du}\frac{du}{dx}$.
